Hello!

I'm Marisa, and I'm a software engineer, full-stack web developer, and fiction and non-fiction writer. I use both sides of my brain and this unique blend of skills brings a special perspective to my work. When I'm not behind a computer screen, furiously typing, I love reading, hiking, and playing with my cats Scorch and Finn.

Currently, I'm working as a software engineer for Performance Software, where I work with an agile team to develop a Python testing suite for safety-critical embedded software for the avionics industry. Previously, I worked as a software engineer for MIT Lincoln Laboratory, Genius Plaza, and GlobalFoundries and graduated from Rochester Institute of Technology with a BS in computer engineering and a minor in creative writing.
